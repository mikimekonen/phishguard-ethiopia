# Privacy Notice (Baseline)

## Purpose
PhishGuard-ET processes security telemetry and user-provided reports to detect phishing threats. This notice summarizes how data is collected, used, retained, and protected.

## Data We Collect
- Detection inputs (URLs, SMS text, email content excerpts)
- Evidence files uploaded by analysts
- Security logs (IP address, request IDs, timestamps)
- Administrative activity logs (audit trails)

## How We Use Data
- Analyze content for phishing indicators
- Investigate cases and document outcomes
- Generate compliance reports and exports
- Improve detection and threat intelligence

## Legal Basis
Processing is required for security operations and incident response. Consent is obtained where applicable for user-submitted reports.

## Data Retention
- Evidence and case artifacts are retained for a defined period (see retention policy).
- Export files are retained for a limited duration and then purged.

## Data Sharing
Data is not shared externally unless required by law or regulatory requests.

## Security Controls
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Audit logs with hash integrity
- Rate limiting and secure storage paths

## Your Rights
You may request access, correction, or deletion subject to compliance obligations and security requirements.
