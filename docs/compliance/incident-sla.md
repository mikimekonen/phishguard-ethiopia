# Incident Response SLA (Baseline)

## Severity Levels
- **Critical**: Active compromise or widespread impact
- **High**: Confirmed phishing with material risk
- **Medium**: Suspicious activity under investigation
- **Low**: Informational or low-risk alerts

## Response Targets
- **Critical**: Acknowledge within 30 minutes, contain within 4 hours
- **High**: Acknowledge within 2 hours, contain within 24 hours
- **Medium**: Acknowledge within 8 hours, contain within 3 business days
- **Low**: Acknowledge within 2 business days

## Escalation
- Analyst → Senior Analyst → SOC Lead → Security Officer

## Communication
- Provide case status updates at least once per business day for open incidents
- Notify stakeholders on containment and closure

## Closure Criteria
- Evidence collected and validated
- Actions documented in case notes
- Final report exported and archived
